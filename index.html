Healer

I designed and implemented a software agent using C++ to tackle a POMDP (Partially Observable Markov Decision Process) problem. The agent's purpose is to recommend sequential intervention plans for homeless shelters, specifically targeting events aimed at raising awareness about HIV among homeless youth. The software agent takes into account the uncertainty and partial observability of the environment to make informed decisions and optimize the impact of the intervention plans on the target audience.
